Dragon Types

Dragon

Largest archtype. Dragons have two wings and 4 legs. Highly intelligent sapient creatures and very long lived. They have their own language, and are able to communicate with humans.

Wyvern

Wyvern are a less intelligent variant of dragons. They have two hind legs and 2 wings in the place of fore limbs, similar to birds.

Drake

Drakes are 4-legged dragonkin with no wings. They can be seen as a draconic version of a horse, in that they are trainable and intelligent, but lack the cognative abilities humans and dragons.

Wyrm

Wyrms are leg-less and wingless dragonkin. Much like serpents and similar to Basilisk. They are inteligent but not typically trainable, and act mostly in instinct and impulse. Similar to many non-domesticated wild animals.

Glossary

  • DragonKin Colloquial term for the taxonomic family all Dragons belong to.
  • Thunder Collective noun for a group of Dragons or Wyvern
